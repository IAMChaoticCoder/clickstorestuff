What's the Pilot?

You mean I'm special?

The pilot is a market evaluation to determine how well the service will work in your area. Storage On Demand™ is backed by UPS, and is integrated into the UPS network. During this time, you will be able to have your items picked up by UPS, and stored at our facility for the remainder of the pilot period. Once the evaluation is complete, we will offer you 3 months of storage at no cost!

The pilot will also be evaluating particular boxes for weight, durability and ease of use. For this reason, we will only be trying out a medium size box listed in the FAQ below.
