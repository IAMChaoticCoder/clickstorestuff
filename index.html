We’re here to provide you a digitally enabled storage solution that removes the hard work and time spent from the traditional storage model, replacing it with ease of use, plenty of options, and all the flexibility you need to support your busy life.

  • You Choose Boxes

    Order packing kits (1-4 per customer)
    Each kit includes:
    - 5 SmoothMove Prime boxes
    - Bubble wrap and packing tape
    - Desiccant packets and padded sheets
    - Large plastic bags to line each box
    - Security Labels and instructions

  • We Deliver

    Labeled boxes and packing materials to your home or office

  • You Pack

    At your pace during the weeks leading up to the pilot pickup dates. If you have questions see more info

  • You Choose Storage

    You pick if you want 30, 60 or 90 days of storage.

    Pilot pricing for storage: $5 per box per month (Including pickup and delivery)
  • Pilot Sign Up

  • We Pickup

    Request a pickup from your home or business during one of the pilot pickup weeks by day and time

  • We Store

    We transport your things safely to a secure UPS facility in your area. You only pay for the exact amount of space you need and don’t have to deal with wasted space in a dusty storage facility.

  • You Decide

    Interact with your boxes as you choose. Request your boxes be returned, or sent to another location of your choosing.

  • We Return

    At the end of your selected storage period we return all of your things to your home or office.

  • We Thank You

    When the full service is live in your area we will provide you free storage equal to the number of months you purchased for pilot storage including free pickup and delivery as a thank you for being a pilot customer!
